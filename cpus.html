the best CPUs for gaming in 2023

Welcome to our page, dedicated to providing you with all the information you need to find the best CPUs for gaming. Our team of experts has researched and tested the latest CPUs on the market to bring you comprehensive reviews and recommendations. That's why we've created a user-friendly list that provides you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

number 6

Starting off, we have the I3 12100F. Given its price point, this chip delivers exceptional gaming performance and good-enough performance in productivity applications due to its great single core performance. This CPU comes with 4 processing cores and 8 threads that operate at a 3.3 GHz base and 4.3 GHz boost clock rate, but it doesn't come with e-cores for background tasks as the Core I5 models do. It also comes with 12 MB of L3 cache.

Don't let the I3 tag fool you because this intel cpu can be paired with up to a RTX 3060 or a RX 6600 without bottlenecking it.

number 5

In 5th place we have the I5 12400F, a much more powerful CPU that delivers exceptional gaming performance and great performance in productivity applications because this CPU comes with 6 processing cores and 12 threads that operate at up to 4.4 GHz boost clock rate and 18 MB of cache.

This powerful budget cpu can be paired with up to a RTX 3070ti or a RX 6700 without bottlenecking it.

check price

number 4

Next, the Ryzen 5 5600, arguably the best budget CPU for under $200 that delivers all the gaming performance that regular gamers should need and really good performance in productivity applications. This CPU comes with 6 processing cores and 12 threads that operate at 3.5 GHz base and 4.4 GHz boost clock rate and 32 MB of cache.

This powerful budget CPU can be paired with up to a RTX 3080ti or a RX 6800 without bottlenecking it.

check price

number 3

In 3rd place, the I5 13600K, a great CPU overall that delivers outstanding gaming and productivity performance. This CPU comes with 6 processing cores and 8 efficiency cores that operate at up to 5.1 GHz boost clock rate and 24 MB of cache, it is also able to overclock to 5.7 GHz.

This powerful CPU can be paired with up to a RTX 3090ti or a RX 6900XT without bottlenecking it.

check price

number 2

In 2nd place we have the Ryzen 7 5800X3D, one of the best CPUs for gaming due to it's 3D cache made for gaming. This CPU comes with 8 cores and 16 threads that operate at 3.4 GHz base clock speed and 4.5 GHz boost clock rate and 96 MB of cache.

This powerful CPU can be paired with up to a RTX 3090ti or a RX 6900XT without bottlenecking it.

check price

number 1

In 1st place, the I9 13900K, the best CPU for gaming and productivity. This CPU comes with 8 performance cores, 16 efficiency cores and 32 threads that operate at 3.0 GHz base clock speed and 5.8 GHz boost clock rate and 32 MB of cache.

This powerful CPU can be paired with any GPU without bottlenecking it.
